Ted Lasso

Ted Lasso is an Apple TV+ comedy-drama series following a relentlessly optimistic American football coach hired to manage an English soccer team despite having no experience. Played by Jason Sudeikis, Lasso uses kindness, humor, and a focus on personal growth to win over skeptical players, fans, and the team’s owner, changing the club culture.
