GeoStorm

Following a series of devastating natural disasters, world governments unite to create "Dutch Boy," a complex, interconnected network of satellites designed to control the global climate and protect the planet. When the system starts malfunctioning, causing extreme weather events instead of preventing them, its creator, Jake Lawson (Gerard Butler), must race against time to fix the program before a worldwide "geostorm" wipes out humanity. The plot involves a conspiracy, with the system having been weaponized, forcing a split narrative between space and Earth as the malfunction is investigated.
